About US

RS ENERGYA was established by the senior management team from a reputable global EPC firm, bringing over 30 years of broad project expertise in the Oil & Gas, Power, and Industrial markets.

Our diverse engineering expertise allows us to provide a full range of domestic and international multi-discipline engineering, procurement, and project management services.
