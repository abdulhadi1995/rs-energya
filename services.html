Our services

RS ENERGYA engineering and project services range from consulting and feasibility studies of small projects to comprehensive project services for large engineering, procurement and construction (EPC) projects.

RS ENERGYA provides engineering services for green and brown field sites, retrofits, revamps, and plants improvements and expansions.

RS ENERGYA' services also include project management consulting (PMC) and owner engineering support.

VALUE CHAIN

At RS ENERGYA, we provide full engineering services focused on adding-value to the project life-cycle from initiation, throughout all phases of engineering, procurement and construction up-to plant operation.

VALUE IMPROVEMENT PRACTICES

RS ENERGYA advocates Value Engineering (VE) reviews and Value Improvement Practices throughout the project phases. These reviews are attended by subject experts and representatives from the owner and the project team such as engineers, project managers, procurement specialists, construction and safety representatives and others. The overall benefits attained include:

  • Process Simplification
  • Cost Reduction
  • Waste Minimization
  • Improved Constructability
  • Enhanced Reliability
  • Predictive Maintenance
  • Design-for-Capacity
  • Optimization of Energy & Utilities requirements

INTEGRATED ENGINEERING

RS ENERGYA provides complete multi-discipline engineering services including, Process, HSE, Mechanical, Piping & Layout, Civil / Structural / Architectural, Electrical and Instrumentation & Control.

At the core of RS ENERGYA is our Process and HSE, driving our design process and supporting disciplines. All areas of engineering design are a key piece of the overall puzzle and ultimate plant design, and the closely coordinated team at STONE ensures that project designs are delivered with the highest possible results.

TECHNOLOGY EXPOSURE

The RS ENERGYA team's collective experience includes Refinery, Petrochemicals, Polymers, Sulfur Recovery, Offsites & Utilities and Inorganic open art and licensed technologies. Evaluation and selection of licensors are critical for many projects as selection of a technology that is deficient in terms of availability, efficiency, yields, catalyst/adsorbent costs, and/or flexibility will reduce profit margins.

RS ENERGYA' structured approach of technology evaluation is based on three technology aspects; process, environmental, and commercial. Our approach enables us to put together optimum solutions which meet our client's requirements.

The RS ENERGYA team understands that competing licensors may quote yield, CAPEX, OPEX, royalties and utilities using different reference points. For this reason, our team eliminates differences in licensors design basis in order to obtain the actual CAPEX and OPEX costs of competing technologies.
